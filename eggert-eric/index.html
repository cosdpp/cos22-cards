Bio

As a consultant on accessibility and inclusion, I support designers, developers, project managers, and business owners to achieve accessibility for their products and services.

I believe that accessibility can drive innovation when it’s considered a fundamental quality of the product. While everyone benefits from an accessible product or service, access to information and participation is a human right.
