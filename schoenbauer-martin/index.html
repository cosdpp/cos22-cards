About me

I´m a 35 year old visual artist, a creative mind and a professional, always waiting for the perfect moment to capture. For me photography is a language more universal than words.

I love to work with people but don´t let my passion for black and white portrait photography fool you; my work is diversified and multifarious.

„Look and think before opening the shutter. The heart and mind are the true lens of the camera.“

I am open to any kind of commercial work which fits my style.
